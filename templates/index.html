I have had the chance to experience all three vehicles and I have the X3MC as my daily driver.

The C63 S AMG sounds amazing and combines both luxury and performance to deliver the ultimate driving machine.

The Corvette, with its mid-engine design, is very unique and comparable to a Ferrari with a much lower price tag. It's beautiful on the outside, but needs some work on the inside.

The X3 M Competition is for those who want a sports car, but need a SUV. The vehicle is great, but needs some fine-tuning and quality control as the 2020 X3MC has been a disappointment.

My Ranking of the Three Vehicles - Best to Worst

  1. C63 S AMG (Coupe)
  2. Corvette
  3. X3 M Competition
